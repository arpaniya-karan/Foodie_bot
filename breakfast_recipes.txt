Recipe Name: Bread Omelette
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Continental
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: bread (2 slices), eggs (2), onion (1 small), green chilli (1), salt, pepper, butter, coriander leaves
Steps:
1. Beat eggs in a bowl. Add chopped onion, green chilli, coriander, salt and pepper. Mix well.
2. Heat butter on a pan over medium flame.
3. Pour the egg mixture and let it set slightly.
4. Place bread slices on top of the semi-cooked egg.
5. Flip carefully so egg side is now on top.
6. Cook for 1-2 more minutes until egg is fully cooked.
7. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
Notes: Great for quick mornings. You can add cheese on top for extra flavor.

Recipe Name: Poha
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: flattened rice/poha (2 cups), onion (1), potato (1 small), mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilli (1), turmeric (1/4 tsp), salt, lemon juice, coriander, oil
Steps:
1. Rinse poha in water and drain. Set aside for 5 minutes to soften.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add curry leaves, green chilli, and chopped onion. Sauté until soft.
4. Add diced potato. Cook covered for 5 minutes on low flame.
5. Add turmeric and salt. Mix well.
6. Add softened poha and mix gently.
7. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Squeeze lemon juice on top.
8. Garnish with coriander and serve.
Notes: Add peanuts for crunch. A classic Indian breakfast that's light and filling.

Recipe Name: Oats Bowl
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Continental
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: rolled oats (1 cup), milk (1.5 cups), banana (1), honey (1 tbsp), cinnamon (a pinch), nuts (optional)
Steps:
1. Pour milk into a saucepan and bring to a gentle boil.
2. Add oats and stir. Reduce flame to low.
3. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thick and creamy.
4. Pour into a bowl. Top with sliced banana.
5. Drizzle honey on top and sprinkle cinnamon.
6. Add nuts if desired and serve warm.
Notes: You can also use water instead of milk. Add fruits of your choice. Healthy and filling.

Recipe Name: Cheese Egg Sandwich
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Continental
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: bread (4 slices), eggs (2), cheese slice (2), butter, salt, pepper, ketchup
Steps:
1. Scramble eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
2. Heat butter in a pan. Pour eggs and scramble on medium flame until just set.
3. Toast bread slices lightly with butter.
4. Place scrambled egg on one bread slice.
5. Add a cheese slice on top.
6. Cover with another bread slice.
7. Press gently and serve with ketchup.
Notes: You can add veggies like capsicum or tomato to the scrambled eggs.

Recipe Name: Pancake
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Continental
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: all-purpose flour (1 cup), egg (1), milk (1 cup), sugar (2 tbsp), baking powder (1 tsp), salt (a pinch), butter
Steps:
1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2. In another bowl, whisk egg and milk together.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir gently until just mixed (small lumps are fine).
4. Heat a pan on medium flame and add a little butter.
5. Pour a small ladle of batter onto the pan.
6. Cook until bubbles form on surface (about 2 minutes). Flip.
7. Cook other side for 1 minute. Repeat with remaining batter.
8. Serve with honey, jam, or fruit.
Notes: Do not overmix the batter or pancakes will be tough. Great weekend breakfast!
