Recipe Name: Veg Sandwich
Meal Type: Snacks
Cuisine: Continental
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: bread (4 slices), cucumber (1/2), tomato (1), onion (1/2), capsicum (1/4), green chutney, butter, chat masala, salt, cheese slice (optional)
Steps:
1. Apply green chutney on one bread slice and butter on another.
2. Slice cucumber, tomato, onion, and capsicum thin.
3. Layer veggies on the chutney slice.
4. Sprinkle chat masala and salt on veggies.
5. Add cheese slice if desired.
6. Cover with the buttered slice.
7. Press lightly and cut diagonally. Serve.
Notes: You can toast in a sandwich maker for a grilled version. Great tiffin snack.

Recipe Name: Chilli Potato
Meal Type: Snacks
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Spicy
Ingredients: potato (3 large), onion (1), capsicum (1/2), garlic (3 cloves), soy sauce (1 tbsp), chilli sauce (2 tbsp), tomato ketchup (1 tbsp), cornflour (2 tbsp), salt, pepper, oil, spring onion, vinegar (1 tsp)
Steps:
1. Cut potatoes into fingers/wedges. Coat with cornflour and salt.
2. Deep fry or air fry until golden and crispy. Set aside.
3. Heat oil in a pan. Add minced garlic.
4. Add chopped onion and capsicum. Sauté for 2 minutes.
5. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, ketchup, and vinegar. Mix.
6. Add fried potatoes and toss well to coat.
7. Add salt and pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
8. Garnish with spring onion. Serve hot.
Notes: Serve immediately to keep potatoes crispy. Crowd favorite at parties!

Recipe Name: Fried Potato Snack
Meal Type: Snacks
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Medium
Ingredients: potato (2 large), salt, chilli powder (1/2 tsp), cumin powder (1/2 tsp), chat masala (1/2 tsp), oil for frying, lemon juice
Steps:
1. Peel and slice potatoes into thin rounds or strips.
2. Soak in cold water for 10 minutes. Pat dry completely.
3. Heat oil in a pan on medium-high flame.
4. Fry potato slices in batches until golden and crispy.
5. Drain on paper towel.
6. While hot, sprinkle salt, chilli powder, cumin powder, and chat masala.
7. Squeeze lemon juice on top. Toss and serve immediately.
Notes: Dry the potatoes well before frying to avoid oil splatter. Serve with green chutney.

Recipe Name: Paneer Roll
Meal Type: Snacks
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Medium
Ingredients: chapati or roti (2), paneer (150g), onion (1), capsicum (1/2), green chutney, chilli sauce, chat masala, salt, oil, lemon juice, coriander
Steps:
1. Cut paneer into small cubes or strips.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Add sliced onion and capsicum. Sauté for 2 minutes.
3. Add paneer and toss on high flame until lightly golden.
4. Add salt, chat masala, and lemon juice. Mix.
5. Warm roti/chapati on tawa.
6. Apply green chutney and chilli sauce on roti.
7. Place paneer filling in the center.
8. Sprinkle coriander. Roll tightly and serve.
Notes: You can add raw sliced onion and cucumber inside for crunch. Great street food at home.

Recipe Name: Upma
Meal Type: Snacks
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Spice Level: Mild
Ingredients: semolina/rava (1 cup), onion (1), green chilli (2), carrot (1 small), peas (1/4 cup), mustard seeds, curry leaves, ginger (small piece), urad dal (1 tsp), oil or ghee, salt, water (2.5 cups), lemon juice, coriander
Steps:
1. Dry roast rava in a pan on low flame until slightly golden. Keep aside.
2. Heat oil in the pan. Add mustard seeds. Let splutter.
3. Add urad dal and roast until golden.
4. Add curry leaves, ginger, green chilli, and onion. Sauté until soft.
5. Add carrot and peas. Cook for 2 minutes.
6. Add 2.5 cups of water. Add salt. Bring to boil.
7. Slowly add roasted rava while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
8. Cover and cook for 3 minutes on low flame.
9. Squeeze lemon juice. Garnish with coriander. Serve with chutney.
Notes: Roasting rava is the key step — don't skip it. Prevents lumps in upma.
