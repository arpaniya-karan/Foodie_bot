Recipe Name: Tomato Omelette
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients: egg, tomato, salt, pepper, oil
Steps:
1. Chop tomato into small pieces.
2. Beat egg with tomato, salt, and pepper.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Pour the mixture and cook both sides.
5. Serve hot.
Notes: Onion and coriander can be added, but they are optional.

Recipe Name: Egg Tomato Scramble
Meal Type: Breakfast
Cuisine: Indian
Cooking Time: 12 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients: egg, tomato, salt, pepper, oil
Steps:
1. Heat oil in a pan.

3. Add beaten egg.
4. Stir continuously until scrambled.
5. Add salt and pepper.
Notes: Onion is optional.
